about
me

Hi there! I'm Angela, and I am currently in my final year at the University of Pennsylvania. This spring I will graduate with a BSE in Digital Media Design, a fusion between Computer Science and Computer Graphics techniques.

When I'm not busy coding, I love to create things and travel! I love all things crafting and also love experimenting with photography especially while travelling. I aspire to find that perfect niche incorporating my passions and skills. Keep scrolling to learn more!
